Refund & Guarantee Policy

Last updated: 17 October 2025

Our outcome guarantee

“10 accepted quotes in 7 days or we keep working free until you do.” After go-live, if you send real quotes and keep your details accurate but do not reach 10 accepted quotes within 7 days, we’ll continue working at no additional cost until the threshold is met.

Change of mind

  • Before kickoff: full refund of any deposit on request.
  • After kickoff but before go-live: pro-rata refund of the setup fee for work not yet performed (we’ll outline what’s been completed).

Project not delivered

If we fail to deliver the agreed scope within a reasonable time after kickoff and you do not wish to continue, we will refund the setup fee you paid.

Payments & fees

  • All prices are in AUD; GST applies if applicable.
  • Third-party processing fees (e.g., Stripe) may be non-refundable according to the provider’s policy.

How to request a refund

Email billing@tradeanchor.com.au with your business name, contact email, invoice number, and the reason for your request. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

Exceptions

Refunds don’t apply where delays or outcomes are caused by missing access, inaccurate data, non-payment, or changes you request beyond the agreed scope.

This policy is in addition to your rights under Australian Consumer Law.
