What we collect

  • Lead info from website forms: name, business, email, phone, trade, and UTM/source.

  • To comply with law and prevent abuse.

Where data lives

  • Google Workspace (Sheets, Docs, Drive, Calendar) – your operational data stays in your Google account.
  • Stripe – payment processing and receipts.

  • Make.com – workflow automation between the above systems.
  • Plausible – anonymous website analytics.

Legal basis

We process data with your consent (form submits), to perform a contract (delivering quotes/acceptance/notifications), and for legitimate interests (product analytics, preventing abuse).

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with the processors listed above and as required by law.

Retention

  • Lead/contact data: until you ask us to delete it or it’s no longer needed for the purpose collected.
  • Operational logs: typically 12–24 months for support/analytics.
  • Invoices/financial records: per Australian record-keeping laws.
